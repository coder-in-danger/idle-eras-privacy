## Overview
Idle Eras: Rebuild Humanity ("the Game") is developed by NovaSpark Interactive. This
policy explains what information the Game collects, how it is used, and your choices.
The Game is designed to be safe for players of all ages, including children under 13,
and is submitted under Google Play's Families Policy.

## Information We Collect
The Game does not require account creation, login, or any personal information to play.

**Locally stored data (stays on your device):**
- Game progress: Sparks balance, era/planet progress, upgrade levels, unlocked managers
- Settings: audio volume, haptics preference, notification preference
- This data is stored in a local save file on your device and is never transmitted to
  NovaSpark Interactive or any third party.

**Advertising data (via Google AdMob):**
- The Game displays ads through Google AdMob to support free-to-play access.
- The Game is configured as child-directed under COPPA. This means AdMob serves
  non-personalized ads only: no behavioral tracking, no interest-based targeting, no
  persistent advertising identifiers are used to build a profile of the player.
- Google AdMob's own data practices are described at
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

**Notifications:**
- If enabled in Settings, the Game schedules local device notifications (offline
  earnings reminder, daily login reminder). These are generated and scheduled entirely
  on-device and are not sent from any external server.

## What We Do NOT Collect
- No account registration, email, or phone number
- No location data
- No camera, microphone, or contacts access
- No in-app chat or user-generated content shared with other players
- No behavioral advertising profile

## Children's Privacy (COPPA)
This Game is directed at a general audience including children. In compliance with
COPPA, all ad requests are tagged as child-directed, which disables personalized
advertising and behavioral tracking for all players, regardless of age.

## Data Retention and Deletion
All gameplay data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the Game permanently
deletes this data. NovaSpark Interactive does not retain any copy of this data on any
server, because none is ever transmitted to us.

## Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated as the Game adds features (for example, optional in-app
purchases). Material changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last updated" date.
